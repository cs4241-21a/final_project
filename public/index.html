Welcome to Paint

Instructions

For Drawing a Line:
  1. Select a color
  2. Select line width
  3. Click and drag on the canvas to make a line
For Erasing:
  1. Select line width
  2. Click and drag on the canvas
For Drawing a Shape:
  1. Select a color
  2. Select a shape
  3. Toggle the Fill Shape checkbox to enable or disable shape fill
  4. Drag the slider to the desired shape size
  5. Click on the canvas to draw the shape
For clearing the canvas:
  • Click the Clear button
For Collaborating With Others:
  1. Share the link: mspaint.edit2014.com
  2. Have them log in and start drawing!
For Logging Out of the App:
  • Click the X button in the upper-right corner of the app window
